~Sleep~

We offer four different accomodation options on our premises. All feature luxurious facilities and beautiful architecture.

Micro

Our most affordable yet memorable option. Fully immersed in nature and energy efficient with a beautiful sunset view. Perfect for adventurous couples.

Macro

A slightly larger option for those planning a longer-term stay. Features a full kitchenette and wonderful treetop scenery.

Mega

Only steps away from white sands and beautiful blue waters. Our Mega accomodations offers an outdoor barbecue pit and scenic surroundings.

Ultra

Our largest accomodation that can fit up to 8 people. Perfect for parties or group travel.
